// BEFORE A ROUND — 10 MIN
Punch shots — wake the body up, find contact 10 balls
Open the Door — rehearse trail arm feel 5 swings
Feet Together — find rhythm and balance 5 swings
Full shots building to driver — execute, don't fix 10 balls
⚡ BLACKBURN RULE
"Position over perfection." Before a round you are EXECUTING not building. Pick one feel and commit to it. Don't try to fix your swing on the tee.
// THE RULE
Maximum 2 thoughts on the course. Ideally 1. These are your go-to options — pick the one that's most relevant to how you're feeling that day. Don't mix them.
// BACKSWING THOUGHTS
1
Open the door
Trail arm swings back and around — low, wide, soft elbow. Stops arm lift at the root.
2
Turn my shirt buttons away
Ribcage coils, not just shoulders. Prevents the fake turn that causes lifting.
// TRANSITION THOUGHTS
3
Float it, then fire
Feel the club levitate at the top before the hips go. Fixes rushing and casting.
4
Butt stays back
Hips rotate, they don't thrust. Chest stays down. Direct fix for topping and thinning.
// IMPACT THOUGHTS
5
Dump the club behind me
Butt of club to the horizon on the way down. Shallows it and removes the flip.
6
Thumbs down through impact
Torso leads, clubhead arrives last. Compressed, low, controlled ball flight.
// WHEN NOTHING'S WORKING
🔄
Feet together — find the rhythm
Take a practice swing with feet together before a difficult shot. Resets tempo and balance instantly.
📈Early Extension — Topping & Thinning
Hips thrust toward the ball in the downswing, spine angle rises, swing arc moves up with your body. Clubhead catches the top or equator of the ball. Most common cause of topped woods and thin irons.
USE THESE DRILLS
🦵 Hip Bump 👊 Punch Shots 👟 Stand Closer 👣 Feet Together
↗️Over the Top — Pull / Slice
Downswing starts with the shoulders or arms rather than the lower body. Club approaches the ball on an outside-in path. Results in pulls, slices, and weak glancing contact.
USE THESE DRILLS
🔻 Dump the Club Out 🦵 Hip Bump 🚶 Step Through ⏸️ Levitation
⚡Arms Lifting — High Hands at Top
Arms go up rather than around in the backswing. Hands end up above the head. Creates a steep downswing, loss of width, and forces the body to compensate at impact.
USE THESE DRILLS
🚪 Open the Door 🎽 Shirt Buttons 🧤 Towel Drill ↔️ Long & Wide
🏃Fast Tempo — Rushing Transition
Transition from backswing to downswing happens too quickly. Upper body fires before the lower body has shifted. Results in loss of lag, thin or fat contact, and inconsistency.
USE THESE DRILLS
⏸️ Levitation 👣 Feet Together 👊 Punch Shots 🚶 Step Through
